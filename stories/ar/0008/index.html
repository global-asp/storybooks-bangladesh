مَاذَا تَفْعَلُ؟ What are you doing?

Nina Orange

Wiehan de Jager

Heba Tesheh, Maaouia Haj Mabrouk

Mashael Muhanna

Arabic

Level 1

أَنَا أُغَنِّي.

I am singing.

هِيَ تُؤَشَّرُ.

She is waving.

أَنَا أُصَفِّقُ.

I am clapping.

هِيَ تَشُدُّ.

She is stretching.

هُوَ يُنَادِي.

He is calling.

أَنَا أُجِيبُ.

I am answering.

هِيَ تَسْمَعُ.

She is listening.

مَاذَا تَفْعَلُ أَنْتَ؟

What are you doing?

Written by: Nina Orange
Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Translated by: Heba Tesheh, Maaouia Haj Mabrouk
Read by: Mashael Muhanna
