What are you doing? তুমি কি করছ? What are you doing?

Nina Orange

Wiehan de Jager

Darshan Soni

English

Level 1

I am singing.

আমি গান গাইছি।

I am singing.

She is waving.

সে হাত নাড়ছে।

She is waving.

I am clapping.

আমি তালি দিচ্ছি।

I am clapping.

She is stretching.

সে হাত প্রসারিত করছে।

She is stretching.

He is calling.

সে ডাকছে।

He is calling.

I am answering.

আমি উত্তর দিচ্ছি।

I am answering.

She is listening.

সে শুনছে।

She is listening.

What are you doing?

তুমি কি করছ?

What are you doing?

Illustrated by: Wiehan de Jager
Read by: Darshan Soni
